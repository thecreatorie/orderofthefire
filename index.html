✦ Our Story

Born from the Flame

Order of the Fire is Austin’s premier entertainment collective, bringing world-class fire performance, aerial artistry, and immersive spectacle to the events that demand the extraordinary.

From intimate private celebrations to massive corporate productions, we transform any venue into an unforgettable realm of living art. Every performance is custom-crafted, every moment choreographed to leave your guests breathless.

Based in Austin, Texas — performing across the nation.

✦ The Heart of What We Do

The Fire Arts

Three distinct disciplines, each a mastery of its own — from spinning fire staffs to breathing living columns of flame. Every act is choreographed. Every moment intentional.

Fire Dancing

Poi · Staff · Fan · Multi-Performer

Choreographed poi, staff, and multi-flame performances by artists who have dedicated their lives to the craft. Captivating from 10 feet or 100 — indoors or out.

Scroll to see more

Fire Breathing

Outdoor Only · Show-Stopping · Signature Act

Towering columns of living flame launched from the performer’s own breath. A primal, awe-inspiring act performed exclusively outdoors — the moment that defines the night.

Scroll to see more

Fire Eating

Close-Up · Strolling · Intimate

An intimate and utterly captivating act. The performer brings flame to their lips and extinguishes it within — leaving audiences in stunned, mesmerized silence.

✦ Beyond the Fire

All Performances

A full suite of immersive entertainment arts — from towering stilt walkers to luminous lamp ladies. We build your complete experience.

🎮 Stilt Walkers

Towering, costumed stilt performers that roam your event as living centerpieces — extraordinary for cocktail hours, arrivals, themed events, and Halloween events.

🐍 Snake Performers

Exotic snake charming performances that add an element of primal wonder and mystique. Unforgettable, conversation-starting, and wildly photogenic.

🬔 Lamp Ladies

Our signature Burlesque-inspired Lamp Ladies — elegant, atmospheric performers who bring old-world glamour and surreal wonder to cocktail hours and upscale events.

🦢 Aerial Performances

Breathtaking aerial silks, lyra, and acrobatic rigging acts. We also offer professional rig rentals and full rigging services for your production.

💡 LED Light Shows

High-energy LED performance art with illuminated props, body suits, and synchronized sequences — the perfect solution for indoor venues where fire isn’t permitted.

✨ GoGo & Custom Acts

High-energy GoGo dancers plus bespoke acts tailored to your event concept, brand, or theme. Custom costuming, choreography, and concepts available.
